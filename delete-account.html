Delete Your Account

Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your data. You can permanently delete your CalorieCalc account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account:

  • Open CalorieCalc on your device
  • Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  • Scroll down and tap "Delete Account & Data"
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

✉️ support@caloriecalcapp.com

Please include the email address you used to register. We will process your request within 48 hours.

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, ALL of the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile and credentials
  • All food logs and nutritional data
  • Health metrics (weight, BMI, hydration records)
  • Exercise and fasting logs
  • AI coaching history and chat messages
  • Meal plans, grocery lists, and recipes
  • Achievement badges, XP, and gamification progress
  • Social connections and community posts
  • Mood tracking and supplement logs
  • Subscription history (billing handled by Google Play)

Important Notes

  • This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
  • If you have an active subscription, please cancel it separately through Google Play Store → Subscriptions to avoid future charges.
  • Anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., total app usage statistics) that cannot identify you may be retained.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at support@caloriecalcapp.com.
